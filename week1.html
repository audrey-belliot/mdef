meeting poblenou

We just finished our first MDEF week with a tour of Poblenou with Chiara.
- BiciHub : I went before actually as I was looking for a self repair shop to fix my bike.
- Trash Lab (it is actually a different name but they work with trash to make stuff)
- Plataforma : art gallery, the owner started a project Poblenou Urban District with many other cultural places in the barrio
- and the last one that I really enjoyed : the name was until now ConnectHort but it become BIOMA (a mix between Biom and Mamma). Besides growing food, they have many different projects including cultural projects, they organize festivals, host concerts, have a sustainable architecture experimentation... Every Saturday morning is dedicated to volunteering so I will go check it out next week!
